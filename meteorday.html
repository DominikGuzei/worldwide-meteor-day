About

Come join us for Worldwide Meteor Day — an open house launch event hosted by local Meteor developers all over the world.

The entire international Meteor community is hosting simultaneous meetups on November 6, open to anyone who is curious to know more about the Meteor platform.

You’ll meet developers in your own city who have built things with Meteor, play with the new Meteor examples and Tutorial, and have a live Hangout with a Meteor Core developer.

If you’re already a Meteor developer, Meteor Day is a time to meet other Meteorites, demo what you’ve built, introduce a friend into the community, and just plain celebrate!
